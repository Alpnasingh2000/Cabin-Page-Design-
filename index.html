Palace on Wheels Cabins

The Palace on Wheels cabins are a true example of royalty, representing the grandeur of India’s most luxurious train. This magnificent train comprises a total of 15 fully air-conditioned coaches, each cabin uniquely designed and adorned with elements inspired by the royal rulers of Rajasthan. The names of the cabins are derived from the princely states of the region, ensuring that every detail, from the décor to the furnishings, reflects the charm and regality of Rajasthan’s heritage. Guests are welcomed into a world of royal indulgence where comfort and culture unite seamlessly.

The accommodation on board is divided into four luxurious categories: Deluxe Cabins, Super Deluxe Cabins, Suite Cabins and the Presidential Suite. The Deluxe Cabins offer a graceful blend of traditional aesthetics and modern amenities, while the Super Deluxe Cabins feature spacious interiors, elegant décor, and personalized comforts for a refined experience. The majestic Presidential Suite stands as the epitome of royal living, providing unmatched luxury, exclusivity, and grandeur. Together, these cabins make the Palace on Wheels an unforgettable journey where heritage, comfort, and elegance come together in perfect harmony.

Presidential Suite

The Palace on Wheels offers an ultra-exclusive Presidential Suite, housed in its own dedicated coach. With just one suite of its kind onboard, it promises a truly unmatched and majestic experience. Spanning 240 sq. ft., this suite is a personal palace in motion, crafted for guests who desire space, privacy, and timeless luxury.

Designed to reflect the grace of royal Rajasthan, the suite offers not just comfort—but art, history, and grandeur at every step. The ceiling is adorned with handcrafted Thikri work, a traditional Rajasthani art form where mirror pieces and intricate patterns come together to create a sparkling tapestry of elegance. Beneath your feet lies Vietnam marble flooring, smooth and cool to the touch, adding a sense of richness and sophistication that defines regal living.

Cabin Features:

  • 240 sq. ft. expansive suite with a luxurious king-size bed
  • En-suite bathroom with a jacuzzi and separate shower area
  • Private in-cabin lounge area for relaxation or entertainment
  • Two handcrafted armchairs with a center table, placed near a large window for enjoying drinks with scenic views
  • Wide window for sightseeing during the journey
  • Ceiling decorated with intricate Thikri work, reflecting traditional mirror artistry
  • Flooring made of premium Vietnam marble, enhancing the elegance of the space
  • LED TV for in-suite entertainment

Suite Cabins

The Palace on Wheels has 1 exclusive coach dedicated to the Suite Cabins category (Jhalawar), which includes only two cabins. Each cabin is spacious, covering an area of 180 sq. ft., and is specially designed to provide maximum comfort, luxury, and privacy.

Each Super Deluxe Cabin features a queen-size bed, elegant Rajasthani décor, beautifully crafted furniture, and is fully air-conditioned. It also includes an en-suite bathroom with a modern shower, offering guests complete convenience and privacy.

Luxury amenities such as high-quality toiletries are provided to enhance your royal stay. A large window is positioned right beside the bed, allowing you to enjoy the beautiful views of the changing landscapes throughout your journey.

With just two cabins in this category, the Suite Cabins experience ensures unmatched exclusivity, comfort, and a truly royal experience aboard the world’s No. 1 luxury train.

Deluxe Cabins

The Palace on Wheels features 12 beautifully appointed coaches exclusively dedicated to deluxe cabins, offering a total of 36 deluxe cabins onboard. Each cabin is a luxurious haven, thoughtfully designed to reflect the royal heritage of Rajasthan.

Each deluxe cabin is 131 sq. ft. in size and comes with twin sharing beds, providing a comfortable and elegant setting for guests. The cabin is equipped with an en-suite bathroom with a shower, elegant interiors, and fine amenities, turning every moment into a scenic experience.

Each coach is named after a princely city of Rajasthan—Alwar, Bikaner, Bundi, Udaipur, Dholpur, Dungarpur, Jaisalmer, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Kishangarh, Kota, and Sirohi—offering guests a truly regal experience during their royal journey.
